Overview

This short animation illustrates a figure-eight movement that bees perform to communicate the location of food.

Details & Reflections

The video is a short animation that illustrations a figure-eight movement called the Waggle Dance. It is one of the ways that bees communicate with each other.

I started by writing the script and story boarding the animation. Next, I designed the elements in Illustrator. A few things I kept in mind was seperating the illustrations into different layers, preparing the size to match the video resolution, and having consistent design across each element. I recorded the narration in Audacity, and brought everything into After Effects. While the animation itself is short, after editing frame by frame, it took several hours to complete. I used the transform tool to adjust the position, scale, rotation, and opacity values. I used masks to rotate or shrink large sections.

Animation is a growing in popularity and I see it across various platforms, including videos and different apps. It is engaging but also time consuming. When budgeting to include animation, perhaps the return on investment for learning should also be considered.
