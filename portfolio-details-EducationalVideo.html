Overview

The video provides an overview of the history of the colors Red and Green in Christmas.

Details & Reflections

This video includes multiple still images and one video, with a voice narration and music in the background. I lost my voice so my husband helped me with the narration.

I started by writing the script and cleaning the white noise husband’s narration in Audacity. I used movements available in iMovie, including panning and zooming through the Ken Burns effect. In approaching this video, I wanted the mood to seem like a happy story telling. I primarily used a transition called ‘Page Curl’ to mimic flipping pages and ‘Fade to Black’ to denote the end of a section. I added screen captions to help clarify content.

This medium is a great medium to facilitate learning and it is also very affordable. Softwares such as iMovie and Windows Movie Maker are free. Also, there are many resources to royalty free images and soundtrack.
